## The Name

Sheaf coherence is a concept from algebraic topology and category theory. A sheaf is coherent when local data — defined on small, overlapping regions — assembles consistently into a global structure.

The metaphor is deliberate. Most business failures are not failures of ambition. They are failures of coherence: structures that look clean on paper but do not hold together in operation. Tax planning that ignores governance. Capital architecture that ignores compliance. AI systems that ignore the actual workflow of the business using them.

Sheaf Coherence as a firm exists to build structures that are locally consistent, and therefore globally sound.

Three tiers of engagement:

**Tier 1 — Corporate Foundation**
Corporate secretarial, compliance, and governance setup. The entry point. The foundation on which everything else is built correctly.

**Tier 2 — Operational Advisory (Retainer)**
Bookkeeping, quarterly financial review, and personal advisory on a retainer basis. This is the margin engine and the relationship layer. Clients at this tier get access to the AI systems described below.

**Tier 3 — Fractional CFO / COO**
Full strategic financial and operational advisory. Capital structure, intercompany logic, treasury, pre-transaction cleanup, and process architecture. High-value, selective, long-term.

## The AI Systems Layer

The core product beneath the advisory tiers is a private AI system deployed for each client.

Architecture:
- Client documents and financial records are stored on a dedicated Virtual Machine (DigitalOcean).
- An AI agent reads those documents and provides advice bounded by the client's actual circumstances and current applicable law.
- A Telegram bot serves as the primary interface — meeting founders where they already communicate.

This architecture solves the privacy problem before it becomes an objection. Documents never pass through a generic public API. Each client's environment is isolated. The system knows their company, their jurisdiction, their structure — and only that.

The output is precise because the inputs are real. Not generic. Not templated. Not hallucinated from training data. Bounded, documented, and traceable.
